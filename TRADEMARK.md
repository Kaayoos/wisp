and are not covered by the GPLv3 grant that applies to this repository's
source code (see LICENSE). Copyright and trademark are separate things:
you get broad rights to the code, and narrower rights to the name.

## What you can do

- Fork, modify, study, and redistribute this source code under the GPLv3.
- Build and run your own modified version under any name you like,
  including "Wisp", as long as you're not distributing it to the public.
- Say your project is "based on Wisp" or "a fork of Wisp".

## What we ask you not to do

- Distribute a modified build to the public under the name "Wisp" or
  using the Wisp logo in a way that could be mistaken for an official
  MinimalPulse release.
- Use the Wisp name or logo to imply endorsement, affiliation, or
  official status you don't have.

If you're building something on top of Wisp and aren't sure whether a
name or use of the logo is fine, email contact@minimalpulse.com. We'd
rather answer a quick question than have a misunderstanding later.
